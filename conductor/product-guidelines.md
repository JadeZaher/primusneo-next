# Product Guidelines

## 1. Design & Branding
- **Visual Identity:** Leverage the existing "PrimusNeo" branding, emphasizing a futuristic, tech-forward aesthetic suitable for Web3 and decentralized applications.
- **3D Integration:** Ensure Spline 3D scenes are seamlessly integrated without compromising page load speeds or accessibility. 3D elements should serve as interactive centerpieces rather than distracting background noise.
- **Color Palette & Typography:** Utilize the Tailwind CSS configuration for consistent theming. Maintain high contrast for readability and use accent colors to drive attention to calls-to-action (CTAs).

## 2. User Experience (UX)
- **Clear Navigation:** The user journey should be intuitive, guiding visitors effortlessly from educational content (blogs, one-pagers) to conversion points (ICO participation, contact forms).
- **Performance:** As a marketing and conversion tool, fast loading times are critical. Optimize assets (images, 3D models) and leverage Next.js Server Components where possible to minimize client-side javascript.
- **Responsiveness:** Ensure a flawless experience across all device sizes, prioritizing mobile-first design principles given the broader consumer audience.

## 3. Content & Prose Style
- **Tone of Voice:** Professional, visionary, yet accessible. Avoid overly dense technical jargon when explaining the Fractal Engine or Fractionalization to the general public, but maintain depth for B2B and investor communications.
- **Clarity & Brevity:** Keep copy concise. Use headings, bullet points, and strong CTAs to break up text and make information easily scannable.

## 4. Accessibility
- **Standards Compliance:** Adhere to WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Ensure all interactive elements are keyboard navigable and semantic HTML is used throughout.
