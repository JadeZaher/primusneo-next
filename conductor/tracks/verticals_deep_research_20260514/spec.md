# Specification: Solution Verticals Deep Research & Copy

## Objective
Transform the existing vertical solution landing pages from placeholders into robust, high-conversion "sticky" pages. This is achieved through deep research into each sector, identifying factual pain points, and crafting copy that highlights PrimusNeo's value proposition using real-world numbers and problem-centric data.

## Scope
- **Sector Research**: Conduct research for each of the 10 defined verticals:
    - Real Estate & Property Development
    - Smart Infrastructure
    - Supply Chain & Logistics
    - Public Governance
    - Web3 & DAO Governance
    - AI & Spatial Intelligence
    - Community & Cooperatives
    - Impact Investing
    - Institutional Investment
    - Developer Ecosystems
- **Copywriting**: Rewrite all solution pages with a focus on:
    - Factual problem highlighting (e.g., specific industry waste metrics, fragmentation costs).
    - Avoidance of made-up statistics like "20% more efficient".
    - "Sticky" headers and clear value-driven sections.
    - Integration of PrimusNeo core layers (AMP, OASIS, NEOS, Fractal Engine) as direct solutions to researched problems.
- **Data-Driven Narratives**: Use verifiable industry data points to anchor the "Why PrimusNeo" argument.

## Constraints
- **No Fake Stats**: Do not invent performance improvements. Use industry-standard problem metrics instead.
- **Brand Consistency**: Maintain the PrimusNeo professional and future-forward voice.
- **Structure**: Leverage the `SolutionTemplate.jsx` for technical consistency while maximizing content depth.
