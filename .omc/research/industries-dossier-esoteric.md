URL: [https://www.ti.org/pdfs/IronLawofMegaprojects.pdf](https://www.ti.org/pdfs/IronLawofMegaprojects.pdf)

- **ENR Top 400 Contractors 2024** — Trade-press margin/concentration data showing the industry's fragmentation isn't just a tech problem; profit-pinching forces consolidation that no twin can fix alone. URL: [https://www.enr.com/articles/58690-enrs-2024-top-400-contractors-firms-feel-pinch-for-profitability](https://www.enr.com/articles/58690-enrs-2024-top-400-contractors-firms-feel-pinch-for-profitability)
